Weiyu AI is the operating model behind an AI-augmented one-person company.

The goal is not to replace human judgment. The goal is to give a single builder a larger surface area for research, engineering, writing, media production, and decision support.

The public side explains the company model. The private side will eventually connect to the command center, agent events, and review queues that help Weiyu decide what should happen next.

## Operating Principles

- Weiyu keeps final approval.
- Agents expand speed and memory, not authority.
- Every important output should leave an artifact.
- Public pages explain concepts; private systems hold execution detail.
